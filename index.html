How to Play

  • Tap a marble (peg), then tap an empty hole two spaces away in a straight line (horizontally or vertically).
  • The marble will jump over an adjacent marble into the empty hole, removing the jumped marble.
  • Keep jumping marbles until no more moves are possible.
  • Try to finish with only one marble left for a perfect game!
  • Use Undo to revert your last move, or Reset to start over.
  • Capture your result with the Screenshot button.
